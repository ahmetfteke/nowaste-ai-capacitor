| Area | Current Issue | Improvement | Metric |
|------|---------------|-------------|--------|

| **Receipt OCR** | Poor performance on faded receipts | Image preprocessing, contrast enhancement | +25% success |

##### 3.3.5 Churn Prevention

**Churn Signals:**

| Signal | Definition | Intervention |
|--------|------------|--------------|

| **No AI usage** | Never used AI features | In-app: AI feature tour |
| **Premium about to cancel** | Cancel button clicked | Survey + offer |
| **Negative feedback** | Low rating in feedback | Personal outreach |

**Churn Prevention Actions:**

| Risk Level | Trigger | Action |
|:----------:|---------|--------|
| ğŸŸ¡ Medium | Opens declining | Helpful content push |

| Report | Frequency | Audience | Key Metrics |
|--------|:---------:|----------|-------------|

- **App Store Analytics:** App Store Connect, Google Play Console
- **User Analytics:** Firebase Analytics, Mixpanel (optional)
- **Revenue Tracking:** RevenueCat Dashboard
- **ASO Monitoring:** App Radar, Sensor Tower, or AppFollow
- **Custom Dashboards:** Amplitude or internal tools
