  General Style Guidelines

  - Line weight: 2-3px, hand-drawn imperfect quality
  - Line color: Stone gray #78716C for outlines
  - Fill style: Flat colors, minimal gradients
  - Shadows: Very subtle, soft, no hard edges
  - Characters: Simple faces (dot eyes, line smiles), minimal limbs
  - Background: All on warm cream #FAF9F6
  - Padding: Content should have ~15% margin from edges
  - Format: PNG with transparent or cream background, 800x800px
